How to make a sandwich.

  1. Gather bread, fillings (e.g., meats, cheese, veggies), and optional condiments.
  2. Spread condiments on bread slices.
  3. Layer fillings on one bread slice.
  4. Place the other bread slice on top.
  5. Optionally, cut the sandwich.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
